// What is Miden

Privacy that works
in the real world.

Miden lets you pick what's public and what's private on a transaction-by-transaction basis. It's designed for businesses that need to keep some things confidential while staying compliant with regulations.

01
Choose Your Privacy
Every transaction, you decide: make it public or keep it private. This flexibility is what makes Miden practical for real businesses that need both transparency and confidentiality.
02
Ready for the Future
Built with quantum-resistant cryptography so your data stays secure even when quantum computers become mainstream. Think of it as future-proofing.
03
Compliance Built In
Privacy doesn't mean hiding from regulators. Miden lets you prove specific things about your transactions without revealing everything, perfect for businesses that need to stay compliant.
